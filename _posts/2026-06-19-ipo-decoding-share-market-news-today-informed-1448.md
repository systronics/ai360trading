## The Numbers That Matter
The IPO GMP (Grey Market Premium) is a crucial metric to watch, as it indicates the demand for the IPO in the grey market. However, it's essential to remember that GMP is not always a reliable indicator of the IPO's performance. I've seen cases where the GMP has been high, but the IPO has listed at a significant discount. To evaluate an IPO, investors should consider the company's financials, promoters, and use of funds. For example, a company with a strong track record of profitability and a clear use of funds is more likely to perform well in the long term.
