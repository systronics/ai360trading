## Country By Country Breakdown
When it comes to term life insurance, the rates vary significantly across countries. For example, in the US, a 30-year-old non-smoker can expect to pay around $25-$30 per month for a $500,000 coverage plan. In the UK, a similar plan might cost between £15-£20 per month. In India, term life insurance plans can start from as low as ₹500-₹700 per month for a ₹1 crore coverage. In Brazil, the rates are somewhat higher, with a 30-year-old non-smoker paying around R$100-R$150 per month for a similar plan. Understanding these rates and comparing them is essential for making an informed decision.
