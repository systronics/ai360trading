3. **How to identify RSI divergence and trade it on the NIFTY?**
RSI divergence occurs when the RSI indicator and the price of the underlying asset are moving in opposite directions. Traders can use this information to identify potential buying or selling opportunities. For example, if the RSI is making a higher high, but the price is making a lower high, it's a bearish divergence, and traders can consider selling.
