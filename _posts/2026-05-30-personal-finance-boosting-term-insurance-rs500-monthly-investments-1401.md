## The Risk Nobody's Talking About
One of the risks that nobody's talking about is the impact of inflation on term insurance coverage. As inflation rises, the value of the coverage amount decreases, which can leave your loved ones with a reduced amount in case of unforeseen events. I'd argue that it's crucial to consider inflation while choosing a term insurance plan and opt for a plan that offers a increasing coverage amount over time. For instance, a plan with a 5% annual increase in coverage amount can help keep pace with inflation.
