## Numbers and Levels
When it comes to investing in cryptocurrencies, the numbers and levels are crucial. The Bitcoin halving cycle, which occurs every four years, can have a significant impact on the value of Bitcoin. The halving cycle is a process where the reward for mining Bitcoin is cut in half, which can lead to increased demand and, subsequently, higher prices. For example, the 2020 halving cycle led to a significant increase in the value of Bitcoin. As noted by the US Treasury, the total value of cryptocurrencies in circulation is estimated to be over $2 trillion.
