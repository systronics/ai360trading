## What Could Go Wrong
One potential pitfall in term life insurance planning is underestimating the coverage amount required. As a general rule, it's recommended to opt for a coverage amount that is at least 10-15 times your annual income. Additionally, it's essential to consider the tax implications of term life insurance premiums and payouts. For instance, in India, term life insurance premiums are eligible for tax deductions under Section 80C, while in the US, term life insurance payouts are generally tax-free. To avoid common mistakes, it's also crucial to review your credit score regularly, as it can impact your insurance premiums and loan interest rates.
