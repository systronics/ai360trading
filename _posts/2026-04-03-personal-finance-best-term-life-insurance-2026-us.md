## India View
In India, the 50-30-20 rule is a great way to start planning your finances. This rule suggests that 50% of your income should go towards necessary expenses like rent, food, and utilities, 30% towards discretionary spending, and 20% towards saving and debt repayment. For example, if you earn Rs. 50,000 per month, you should aim to save Rs. 10,000 and use Rs. 15,000 for discretionary spending.
