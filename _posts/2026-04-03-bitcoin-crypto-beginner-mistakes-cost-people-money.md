## The Risk Nobody's Talking About
One of the biggest risks facing crypto investors is the risk of scams. With the rise of crypto, scammers have become increasingly sophisticated, and it's essential to be aware of the common scams and how to avoid them. For example, phishing scams, where scammers send fake emails or messages to trick investors into revealing their login credentials or wallet addresses, are common. Another risk is the risk of exchange hacks, where hackers gain access to an exchange's servers and steal funds from investors' accounts. To mitigate this risk, it's essential to use a reputable exchange and to enable two-factor authentication. The risk of price volatility is also a significant concern for crypto investors. With prices fluctuating wildly, it's essential to have a solid risk management strategy in place. This includes setting stop-loss orders, diversifying your portfolio, and not investing more than you can afford to lose.
