## The Consensus View (And Why It's Wrong)
Most people believe that the Bitcoin halving cycle is a guaranteed way to make money, with many expecting the price of Bitcoin to skyrocket after each halving event. Lekin, this view is overly simplistic and ignores the complexities of the cryptocurrency market. The Bitcoin halving cycle explained in simple terms is a process where the reward for mining Bitcoin is cut in half, which happens approximately every four years. This reduction in supply is often cited as a key driver of Bitcoin's price, yet it's not the only factor at play. In fact, the relationship between the Bitcoin halving cycle and price is more nuanced, and understanding this nuance is crucial for making informed investment decisions.
