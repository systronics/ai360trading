## Confirming Signals
When it comes to buying crypto safely, it's essential to follow a step-by-step process. First, choose a reputable exchange, such as Coinbase or Binance, and create an account. The Bitcoin halving cycle, which occurs every four years, is another essential concept to understand. The halving cycle reduces the reward for mining Bitcoin by half, which can lead to a decrease in the supply of new Bitcoins. This reduction in supply can lead to an increase in demand, causing the price to rise. For example, the 2020 halving cycle led to a significant increase in Bitcoin's price, making it an attractive investment opportunity.
