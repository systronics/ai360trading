## Trader FAQs
### What is the process to apply for an IPO online in India using ASBA/UPI?
To apply for an IPO online in India, investors can use the ASBA/UPI method. This involves linking their bank account to the UPI platform and using the IPO application form to apply for the IPO. Indian traders can open a free account at [Zerodha](https://zerodha.com/open-account/) to apply for IPOs online.
### How to evaluate an IPO's valuation, financials, promoters, and use of funds?
Evaluating an IPO's valuation, financials, promoters, and use of funds involves analyzing the company's financial statements, management team, and industry trends. Investors should look for companies with strong financials, a proven track record, and a clear plan for using the IPO proceeds.
### What does IPO GMP signal, and what are its limits?
The IPO GMP, or grey market premium, signals the demand for an IPO in the grey market. However, it's essential to understand its limits and not get carried away by hype. The GMP can be volatile and may not reflect the actual listing price.
