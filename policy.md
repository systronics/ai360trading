## 1. Privacy Policy
{: .section-title}

We value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information in compliance with **GDPR (EU/UK)**, **US State Privacy Laws**, and **India's IT Act 2000**.

While using our Service, we do not directly collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., via email contact). However, we use third-party services that may collect data used to identify you, including:

* **Google AdSense:** For serving personalized and non-personalized advertisements based on your browsing behavior.
* **Google Analytics:** To analyze global traffic patterns and improve user experience.
* **Firebase & Cloudflare:** For website infrastructure, performance optimization, and security logging.

### Cookies & Ad Personalization
Google and other third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

* **Opt-Out:** You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or [www.aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info).
* **Consent:** Visitors from the EEA and UK will be presented with a Consent Management Provider (CMP) banner to manage their data preferences.
* **Do Not Track:** We respect browser Do Not Track signals where applicable.

### Data Retention
We do not store personal data on our servers. Any data collected by third-party services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

### Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
* Access your personal data
* Request deletion of your data
* Opt out of data collection
* Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
