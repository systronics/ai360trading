Frequently Asked Questions

Up to what salary is income tax zero in the new regime?
With the ₹75,000 standard deduction and Section 87A rebate, a salaried person earning up to about ₹12.75 lakh pays no income tax.
Does this include the old tax regime?
This calculator uses the New Tax Regime (the default). The old regime allows more deductions and needs to be opted for separately.
Is this calculator official?
No — it is a free estimate for guidance. Always verify with the Income Tax Department or a tax professional, and check the latest Budget for any slab changes.
