How to compress an image to a target KB

Upload a JPG or PNG, then either set a quality level or type a target size in KB. The tool re-encodes the image right in your browser, lowering quality step by step until it reaches your target size. Optionally set a maximum width to resize large photos — perfect for online forms that demand "photo under 100 KB" or "signature under 20 KB".
