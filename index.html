How Our Algo Trading System Works

AI360Trading uses a rules-based algorithmic approach to identify high-probability setups in Nifty 200 stocks. Our system scans for institutional accumulation patterns, volume breakouts, and price structure confirmations before generating a signal. Every setup is verified manually before being published.

We do not rely on lagging indicators or generic moving average crossovers. Our methodology is built on reading raw price action — understanding where large operators are placing orders, and aligning retail trade entries with those institutional footprints.

Swing Trading vs Positional Trading

Our platform covers two distinct trading styles. Swing trading setups are designed for 3 to 15 day holding periods, targeting momentum moves driven by volume and short-term institutional activity.

Positional picks focus on structural shifts in a stock's long-term trend — suited for investors with a 1 to 6 month time horizon. Both dashboards include defined entry zones, profit targets, and stop-loss levels.
