Swing Trading vs Positional Trading

Our platform covers two distinct trading styles to suit different types of traders. Swing trading setups are designed for 3 to 15 day holding periods, targeting momentum moves driven by volume and short-term institutional activity. These signals are ideal for active traders who can monitor positions a few times per week.

Positional picks, on the other hand, focus on structural shifts in a stock's long-term trend. These setups are based on fundamental strength combined with technical confirmation, and are suited for investors with a 1 to 6 month time horizon. Both dashboards are updated regularly and include defined entry zones, profit targets, and stop-loss levels.
